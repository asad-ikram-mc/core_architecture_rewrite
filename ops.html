SCHEMA MISMATCH
When Sprinklr renames a column (like the GCCI→CCR rename in V5), T0 catches it and the pipeline stops. Fix it here.
How Mismatches Happen

Sprinklr periodically renames custom field columns. In V5, all GCCI_ prefixes changed to CCR_ or hex IDs overnight. T0 validates incoming S3 files against expected_structure.py and stops the pipeline with a Slack alert listing exactly which columns are missing.
